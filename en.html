Games

SUMI HOTARU 墨蛍

Draw walls with an ink brush to guide a bouncing firefly to the stars — but your ink never disappears, and slowly becomes your own maze. Every run ends as a unique sumi-e painting on a hanging scroll, which you can save and share.

UI is in Japanese, but the game teaches itself in seconds: draw a line, the firefly bounces, reach the star. Best on iPad with Apple Pencil.

Lab

Prototypes of tools for live streamers — a tiny garden that grows from your chat, and a poll where the options race each other. Each demo has a built-in chat box so you can try the whole loop without setting anything up.

  • Comment Garden — viewers' comments grow grass, bloom flowers, launch fireworks. A quiet chat attracts a cat.
  • Race Poll — type "1" in chat and watch that option sprint.
