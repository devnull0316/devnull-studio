/dev/ad β — working prototype

One ad slot.
Anyone can write to it.

Ad space has always been read-only — you either buy it or you watch it. /dev/ad flips that: one shared slot, free for anyone to post to. Promote your thing, show a drawing, or just say what you had for lunch.

HOW IT WORKS

  • Posting is free. Attention is the currency. Each Boost from a viewer keeps an ad alive 8 more hours.
  • Ignored ads fade out. The signal drops, static creeps in, and the slot moves on. Nothing can squat here.
  • Bigger formats burn faster. Only a well-boosted ad survives full-screen.
  • A human reviews every ad before it goes live. No image uploads (drawing is fine). Links from a short allowlist only.
  • Pixel boards are shared. A few permanent frames where anyone can place pixels, one at a time, together.

SURFACES

  • The same pool can run anywhere: new tab, wallpaper, taskbar ticker, terminal, stream overlay. Try the SURFACE switcher at the bottom.
  • Wallpaper and stream are real today: Lively Wallpaper turns this URL into a live wallpaper, and OBS loads it as a browser source.

This prototype runs entirely in your browser — nothing you post is shared yet. Open Admin to review posts and fast-forward time to watch ads fade out.
